Inspiration

Inspired primarily by Scandinavian textiles and the Swedish practice of lagom (“not too much, not too little”), I am drawn to clean and simple design with bold and whimsical accents.

Projects

Department of the Interior

Hex # F34C1B

The restructuring of the Department of the Interior site navigation and aesthetic. Aiming to enable Users to find information relating to the department’s mission and vision, the offices and bureaus being managed, and relevant current events efficiently.

Open Arms of MN

Hex # FBA91E

The redesign of the homepage and Volunteer sign-up process for the nonprofit Open Arms of Minnesota. Hoping to increase volunteer numbers amidst the growing community need for the services provided by the organization (meals for individuals struggling physically and financially).

Neutral Mobile App

Hex # C9D8DF

The design of a mobile app where Users looking to lessen their carbon footprint can travel carbon neutral. Users can compare methods of travel based on carbon production and cost, and offset their emissions by supporting local carbon negative initiatives.
