02. Definition & Synthesis

Problem Statement

001. "The Department of the Interior site was designed to be a liaison between Users and the United States Bureaus and Offices.”
002. “We have observed that the existing product is difficult to navigate and unorganized, which is causing Users to utilize outside resources and miss important updates.”
003. "How might we improve the Department of the Interior site so our customers are successful based on their ability to find any page in under fifteen seconds."

Feedback & Iteration

Problems
[1] Only see one featured “This Week in the Interior” article on the homescreen.
[2] Have to navigate to the secondary pages to reach the tertiary pages.
[3] Don’t know what tertiary pages exist without a dropdown.
[4] The navigation bar is too large / takes up the majority of the screen.
Solutions
[1] Swipe through multiple featured “This Week in the Interior” articles on the homescreen.
[2] Tertiary pages drop down from the secondary page dropdown.
[3] The navigation bar is reduced in size and takes up less of the screen. 
[4] Arrows indicating tertiary options below.
